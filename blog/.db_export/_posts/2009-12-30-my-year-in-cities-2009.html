Somerville, MA
Boston, MA
Avon, CT
Denver, CO
Killington, VT
New York, NY
Brooklyn, NY
Weehawken, NJ

Not terribly impressive (2008 would've also included London, Rome, Vatican City, & elsewhere in the US) but not too shabby either! I hope 2010 will be better... Boston, DC, and SF will pop up again, hopefully something exotic as well... Alaska? Ireland? Another jaunt about europe? I doubt it. But I can dream, America. I can dream.
