I'm going deep sea squid fishing tomorrow morning! Don't usually do things that are this "hunting"-y, but I figure it's a once-in-a-lifetime thing when a friend asks if you want to charter a boat and hunt 4" squid miles off the coast in the Pacific Ocean.

Also, they're Humboldt Squid, which are apparently supposed to be no further north than LA, but with the warmer-than-normal water, they're swimming all the way up here to SF, and eating everything around. So technically, it is our ecological duty to harpoon them and return balance to the world.
