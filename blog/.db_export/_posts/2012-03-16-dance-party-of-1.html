Gotye - Somebody That I Used To Know, feat. Kimbra (4FRNT Remix)

This is the best remix of Gotye's Somebody That I Used To Know that I've heard. Ashley and I found one mix of the song through a lucky cab ride radio station, and Shazam for iPhone, then went looking for the version we heard. I never did find that radio version, and the original is a little slow for my tastes. But this. This is a new favorite. (Even Gotye himself is a fan)

@4FRNTmusic's mix of Somebody- hands down the craftiest and most exciting dance remix I've heard of the track
https://twitter.com/#!/gotye/status/164536067255582720

 

4FRNT seems to be extremely new, so if you like it there's no high quality download link. But this music blog has the best one I've found. (Not DJ quality, but sounds great in headphones!) http://ilspins.com/somebody-that-i-used-to-know-feat-kimbra-4frnt-remix/
